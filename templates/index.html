Welcome to the Air Pollution Index Prediction

The **Air Pollution Index (API)** is a measure of air quality based on the concentration of pollutants in the atmosphere. It helps determine the level of pollution and its potential impact on human health. Higher API values indicate worse air quality, with categories ranging from **Good** to **Hazardous**.

Use this tool to predict air quality based on key environmental factors. Enter your data, and let the AI determine the pollution level.
